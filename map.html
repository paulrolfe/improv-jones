Directions:

From points North: Take 95 South to exit 21, Atwells Ave. Take a left at the light at the end of the ramp. Go across the bridge through two more lights. At the second light take a left. This street curves to the right. Go through the first two lights. Perishable Theatre is halfway up the next block on your left, 95 Empire St.

From points South: Take 95 North to exit 21, Downtown. Take the first right off the exit ramp. Go through three lights. (The street curves to the right.) Perishable Theatre is halfway up the next block on your left, 95 Empire St.

From points East: Take 195 West to Providence. Merge onto 95 North, then immediately take exit 21, Downtown. Take the first right off the exit ramp. Go through three lights. (The street curves to the right.) Perishable Theatre is halfway up the next block on your left, 95 Empire St.

Shows start at 10:00, Thursdays and Saturdays.
